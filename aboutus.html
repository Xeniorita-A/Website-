Craft is passionately creating something with your hands

Crafting - October 22, 2021

Art and craft have always been appreciated, but their popularity has grown in recent years as people experiment with new techniques, styles, and media in both traditional and contemporary crafts. Scrapbooking, card making, altered art, up-cycling, mixed media, and decoupage are new craft genres that are gaining popularity. Everything can be made with machines, but nothing beats something built with one's own hands and heart.

My name is Jodel Botilo, and I am a craftsman from Palnab, Virac Catanduanes. at first,  I didn't think crafting was my strong suit.  Just like any other young adult out there,  I'm struggling to find decent work. Shortly, the pressure of not finding work became too much to bear. I'm unable to function normally, and I've lost the will to do anything. Not until one of my friends and fellow born-again brother introduced me to crafting. I've rediscovered my passion. I've developed a strong interest in making various handmade products. Starting with Abacca rolls, beads, paper flowers and vases, then progressing to hanging decors made of wood and paracord.

When everything seemed to be falling apart, I turn to my crafting, and I began to dream about opening my own business. Now I am grateful to be able to share this skill and the items that I created with all of my heart. I designed each of these goods with the intention of making my clients happy. Craft, in my opinion, makes our homes more human. I'd like to share these quotes with you in the hopes that they may inspire you to hold on to something that you are passionate about.

“Art is not a handicraft, it is the transmission of feeling the artist has experienced.”- Leo Tolstoy

“No one masters his craft the first day.”- Roman Proverb

Creativity must be unleashed, explored, and expressed. Poets, writers, painters, artisans, and crafters all specialize in a specific type of craft. Each craftfs communicate emotions about creativity, such as how one can utilize one's intellect and heart to capture memories, moods, and sensations in novel ways.

Everything about creativity, whether it's art or crafts, involves feelings. Some of the best craftsman states that creativity can never be exhausted. Something new to discover is always available. Some of the best craft quotes serve as motivation for artists, poets, authors, and craftspeople, as well as anybody interested in the creative arts.
